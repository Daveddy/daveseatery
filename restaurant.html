About Us

Welcome to Dave's Eatery, your home for authentic Ghanaian cuisine and warm hospitality.

Dave's Eatery has been a cherished part of the culinary landscape in Tesano, proudly serving up traditional Ghanaian flavors and dishes passed down through generations. Inspired by the rich culinary heritage of Ghana, our menu features a tantalizing array of local favorites, from hearty stews and aromatic soups to succulent grilled meats and vibrant vegetarian options.
